5Monkey

Classy cuisine deserves a stage

5Monkey is a dining experience in Sonipat that prides itself on quality. The brand and web presence needed to match — refined, confident, and built to last.

Brief

Brand identity from scratch

5Monkey needed more than a website — they needed a brand. We started with a complete identity system: logo design, typography, color palette, and a visual language that communicated refinement without pretension.

The brand needed to feel modern and premium but also approachable. Classy cuisine for people who care about what they eat, not just where they're seen.

CMS & Web Design

Built to be maintained

The website is CMS-powered, giving the 5Monkey team full control over their menu, events, and content without touching code. The editorial layout balances photography with typographic pricing — every dish presented with the attention it deserves.

Pages load fast, feel smooth, and work equally well on mobile. The reservation system integrates without friction — reducing drop-off between intent and booking.

Menu System — CMS-Powered, 2025

Outcome

A brand and digital presence that elevated 5Monkey's positioning in the market. The team can manage content independently, and the site continues to reflect their evolving menu season to season.
